Services

Eight services. One project lifecycle.

From first opportunity screen to post-implementation monitoring — INA advises across the full arc of a project.

01
Screening & Prioritization

Opportunity Identification

Screening and prioritizing digital infrastructure opportunities against strategic, market and policy criteria — before significant resources are committed.

  • Market & demand scanning
  • Strategic fit assessment
  • Early-stage opportunity scoring
  • Go / no-go recommendation
02
INA Project Structuring Framework™

Project Structuring

Applying INA's flagship methodology to move an opportunity from concept toward bankability, across technical, regulatory and financial dimensions.

  • Stage-gated structuring process
  • Technical feasibility review
  • Regulatory & institutional fit
  • Structuring memo & gap list
03
Capital Stack Design

Financial Structuring

Designing capital stacks that balance public policy objectives with private investment logic, across concessional, blended and commercial capital.

  • Capital stack design
  • Cash flow & return modeling
  • Public / private risk allocation
  • Financing term sheet support
04
INA Investment Readiness Index™

Investment Readiness

Benchmarking projects against INA's composite readiness index and closing the highest-priority gaps before approaching capital.

  • Eight-dimension scoring
  • Peer benchmarking
  • Prioritized improvement roadmap
  • Re-assessment as project matures
05
PPP Structuring

Public-Private Partnerships

Structuring PPP arrangements aligned with regulatory frameworks and international risk-allocation best practice.

  • Risk allocation design
  • Contract & concession structuring
  • Regulatory alignment review
  • Bidder engagement support
06
INA AI Advisory Methodology™

AI Advisory

Helping organizations adopt AI responsibly and strategically — from use-case prioritization to governed, monitored deployment.

  • Use-case prioritization
  • Data & infrastructure readiness
  • Governance & ethics charter
  • Adoption roadmap
07
Implementation Governance

Project Monitoring

Tracking implementation against plan with structured dashboards, milestone governance and continuous risk monitoring.

  • Milestone & KPI dashboards
  • Risk register monitoring
  • Stage-gate governance
  • Lender / DFI reporting support
08
Environmental, Social & Governance

ESG Integration

Embedding environmental, social and governance criteria across the project lifecycle, from screening through post-implementation review.

  • ESG screening & due diligence
  • Social & environmental safeguards
  • Governance & reporting structures
  • Impact monitoring
